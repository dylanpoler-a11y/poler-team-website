Investment Thesis

Two properties, one operation, exponential upside

The Lauderdale Boutique Hotel (505) is a stabilized, cash-flowing, 100% automated 17-unit hotel generating $743K actual 2025 revenue with 80% occupancy and $149 ADR — proven numbers from the Lauderdale OM. Directly across the street, 506 SE 16th St is a 14-unit multifamily ready for conversion into 24 additional hotel keys projecting $1.06M Year 1 revenue at 11.53% cap. Combined, the two properties create a 41-key boutique operation on 34,950 SF with shared technology, shared management, and dramatically improved economics through economies of scale.
  • ✓ Proven Playbook: The 505 conversion from 13 outdated motel rooms to 17 tech-enabled boutique keys has already been executed — same concept, copy & paste to 506
  • ✓ Across-the-Street Synergy: Both properties share one operation, one tech stack (Guesty, Conduit AI, PricePoint), one part-time manager — doubling keys without doubling costs
  • ✓ Economies of Scale: Combined 41-key operation unlocks institutional-grade RevPAR, vendor pricing, and OTA positioning that 17 keys alone cannot achieve
  • ✓ Triple Lot Assemblage: 505 sits on 3 contiguous lots (21,450 SF) with 5-story zoning — the expansion lot is 70% vacant and already has design plans
  • ✓ Location Demand Drivers: 2 min from Broward Health (716 beds), walkable to $1.2B Convention Center, 10 min from FLL Airport & Port Everglades
