SMS Communications Policy

The Poler Team

The Poler Team ("we," "us," or "our") uses SMS text messaging to communicate with commercial real estate investors, hospitality industry professionals, and other business contacts about investment property opportunities.

What Messages You'll Receive

By opting in to SMS communications from The Poler Team, you may receive messages about:

  • Commercial real estate investment opportunities in South Florida
  • Property details, pricing, and availability updates
  • Meeting and call scheduling confirmations
  • Follow-up communications regarding properties of interest

How to Opt In

You may opt in to receive SMS messages from The Poler Team by:

  • Submitting the contact form below with your phone number and consent
  • Providing your phone number to a Poler Team representative and consenting to receive texts
  • Responding to an initial outreach message

By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive text messages from The Poler Team regarding commercial real estate opportunities. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

How to Opt Out

You can opt out of SMS communications at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message you receive from us
  • Emailing us at info@polerteam.com with your opt-out request
  • Calling us at (954) 833-5106

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further texts.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your engagement with our team and available properties. Typically, you may receive 1-4 messages per month.

Help

For help or questions about our SMS program, reply HELP to any message, email info@polerteam.com, or call (954) 833-5106.

Privacy

Your phone number and personal information will not be shared with third parties for marketing purposes. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
