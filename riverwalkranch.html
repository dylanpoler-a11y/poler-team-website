Key Risks & Concerns

Large AR Balance: $8.2-9.4M AR flagged as unusually high. Driven by in-network transition timing, but collectability of older claims is a concern.
EBITDA Gap: $2.3M spread between reported ($3.5M) and adjusted ($5.8M). Prior deal blew up possibly due to credit disputes on add-backs.
Valuation Expectations: Seller anchored at 8x from prior LOIs; indicative floor at ~7x. Gap may widen if adjustments aren't validated.
Competitive Process: Family offices also engaged; sellers managing multiple conversations simultaneously.

Strengths & Highlights

Near-Full Occupancy: 99% occupancy with only 1 bed open out of 95. Strong demand validation.
Owned Real Estate: All properties owned, including 24-acre ranch at Riverwalk. No lease risk.
Gold Card Status: Both Riverwalk and Fort Worth Recovery have gold card designation with payers.
Motivated Seller: Angel investor in his 70s, actively pushing for exit. Principals are entrepreneurs with no emotional attachment to "the kingdom."
Expansion-Ready: Detailed plans for San Antonio + additional markets. Built from cash flow with proven execution.
