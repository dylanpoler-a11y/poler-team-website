About The Poler Team

Founded by Rosa Poler with over 20 years of real estate experience, The Poler Team is a family-operated firm based in Sunny Isles Beach, Florida. What began as a residential real estate practice has grown into a multi-division operation spanning M&A deal sourcing, commercial brokerage, and hospitality asset management.

Each member of the team brings a distinct specialty, and together we cover the full spectrum of real estate and business advisory services. We treat every client like a partner, not a transaction. With over 100 successful closings and 500+ satisfied clients, our track record speaks for itself.

Connecting Buyers with Off-Market Acquisition Targets
